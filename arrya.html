✦ General Rules – All Events

🪪 ID Mandatory

Valid College ID Card must be carried at all times during the fest.

⏰ Reporting Time

All participants must report at their event venue at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time.

📋 Registration

Registration via the official link is mandatory. Spot registrations may not be accepted.

🤝 Discipline

Ragging, misconduct, or disrespecting judges/organizers will lead to immediate disqualification.

📱 Devices

Mobile phones must be on silent mode during all performances and sessions.

⚖️ Judges' Decision

The decision of the judging panel is final and binding in all competitions.
